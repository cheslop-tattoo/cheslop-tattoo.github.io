How do I book a tattoo appointment?

You can book an appointment through my online booking form. Click the "Book a Tattoo" button in the navigation menu to access the form. Please provide as much detail as possible about your desired design, size, and placement.

What is your minimum price?

My minimum price is Â£80. This covers small, simple designs that take approximately 1 hour to complete. Larger or more complex designs will be priced accordingly.

Do you do custom designs?

Yes! I specialize in custom designs and love creating unique pieces for my clients. When booking, please provide reference images and describe your vision in detail.

How should I prepare for my tattoo appointment?

Make sure to get a good night's sleep, eat a substantial meal before your appointment, and stay hydrated. Avoid alcohol 24 hours before your appointment. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed.

What is your cancellation policy?

I require 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may result in a deposit being charged for future bookings.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a Â£50 deposit is required to secure your appointment. This is non-refundable but goes towards the final cost of your tattoo.

How do I take care of my new tattoo?

I'll provide detailed aftercare instructions after your appointment. Generally, you'll need to keep the tattoo clean, moisturized, and out of direct sunlight. Avoid swimming, soaking, and picking at the tattoo during the healing process.

Do you do cover-ups?

Yes, I do cover-ups. However, they require careful consideration and planning. Please send photos of the existing tattoo when booking, and we can discuss the possibilities.
